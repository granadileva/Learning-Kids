Privacy Policy


  1. Welcome to Learning Kids ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and their parents while providing a safe and educational online experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information on our learning website for kids.

  2. Information We Collect
    We collect limited personal information to ensure a safe and enriching experience for children. The types of information we may collect include:
    - Personal Information: We collect only the minimum necessary personal data, such as a parent or guardian’s email address for account creation and consent verification.
    - Non-Personal Information: This includes data such as device type, browser type, and general usage statistics to improve our website.

  3. How We Use the Information
    We use collected information to:
    - Provide educational content and services.
    - Enhance user experience and website functionality.
    - Communicate with parents/guardians regarding their child’s use of our services.
    - Ensure compliance with child safety laws.

  4. Parental Consent & Control
    We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and require parental or guardian consent before collecting personal information from children under 13. Parents have the right to review, modify, or delete their child’s information by contacting us at group01@gmail.com.

  5. Information Sharing & Security
    We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties except:
    - With parental consent.
    - When required by law or to protect user safety.
    - With trusted service providers who help operate our website, under strict confidentiality agreements.
    We implement industry-standard security measures to protect all collected data.

  6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies
    We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and track website usage. Parents can manage cookie settings through their browser preferences.

  7. Third-Party Links
    Our website may contain links to third-party educational resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

  8. Changes to this Privacy Policy
    We may update this policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated to parents via email or a notice on our website.

  9. Contact Us
    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at group01@gmail.com.
