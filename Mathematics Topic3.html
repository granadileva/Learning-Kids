MEASUREMENT

Measurement is the process of determining the size, length, weight, volume, or amount of something. It helps us understand and compare different objects and quantities. Common units of measurement include inches, centimeters, pounds, kilograms, liters, and milliliters.

Examples


Measuring Length
Imagine you want to measure the length of a pencil. You can use a ruler to find out how long it is.
If the pencil measures 7 inches, you can say:
  • Length of the pencil = 7 inches
Measuring Weight
If you have a bag of apples and you want to know how heavy it is, you can use a scale.
If the bag weights 3 pounds, you can say:
  • Weight of the bag of apples = 3 pounds
Measuring Volume
If you want to measure how much water is in a bottle, you can use a measuring cup.
If the bottle holds 500 milliliters of water, you can say:
  • Volume of water in the bottle = 500 milliliters
